Margot Menu

Margot is a dynamic menu for restaurants. It is a mobile-first but screen-responsive website.

The concept of Margot is to make the user experience easy and smooth. The flow on Margot is a user will go to the desired restaurant and scan a specific QR code which is provided by Margot which will redirect him to the restaurant's dynamic menu. Choose an item, see the reviews given by other users and order.

Designed as a final project for my Le Wagon bootcamp, Margot was built on Ruby on Rails following the MVC model. Other than Ruby, the project used HTML, CSS/Bootstrap, Javascript, and PostgreSQL. My role on our team of three developers was to focus on design and implement the front-end. I also worked on the back end setting up models, controllers, seeding and routes. The project was presented after ten days of work and is still being updated by the team.
