Surf Stick

Place for Surf Board

Surf Stick is an airbnb clone where you can rent a surfboard from different people. Most people don't want to carry their surfboard around when they are traveling, but with a Surf Stick it makes it easier to get your desired surfboard anywhere you go. It is a mobile based but also screen responsive website.

Surf Stick was the first project in my Le Wagon bootcamp. There were 3 devs on the team and I was pair-programming with another teammate for most of the 5 days it took to develop. We used Ruby on Rails, HTML, CSS/Bootstrap, Javascript, and PostgreSQL to build the website. My focus on this project was setting up the models, controllers, and seeding. The project is still being updated.
